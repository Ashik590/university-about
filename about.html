Background of the university

In order to up-lift the higher education sector in Jamalpur and in Sherpur city, a private university in Jamalpur district named Sheikh Fazilatunnesa Mujib University was established under the auspicious initiative of honorable state minister Mirza Azam MP. The university got approval from UGC, and The Ministry of Education in the year 2013.
